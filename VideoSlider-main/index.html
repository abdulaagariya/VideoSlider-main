INDIA

India is a diverse and culturally rich country located in South Asia. It is known for its ancient history, diverse landscapes, and a population of over 1.3 billion people.

CANADA

Canada is the second-largest country in the world by land area, known for its stunning natural landscapes, including vast forests, rugged mountains, and numerous lakes. It is a diverse nation with a rich cultural tapestry, embracing both English and French as official languages, and is known for its universal healthcare system and reputation for politeness!

Thailand

Thailand, located in Southeast Asia, is renowned for its vibrant culture, beautiful beaches, and delicious cuisine. It boasts a rich history, with ancient temples and modern cities coexisting harmoniously, making it a popular destination for tourists seeking a blend of tradition and modernity.!

Maldives

The Maldives, an island nation in the Indian Ocean, is celebrated for its stunning overwater bungalows, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. This tropical paradise is a popular destination for honeymooners and travelers seeking luxury resorts and world-class snorkeling and diving experiences amidst its picturesque coral ree!
